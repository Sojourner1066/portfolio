About Me

I’m a passionate geospatial professional who thrives at the intersection of people, place, and technology. With over two decades of experience in geospatial data science, remote sensing, and applied analytics, I’ve led teams and initiatives that drive strategic change, improve mission clarity, and foster stronger client relationships. At heart, I’m a geoevangelist—energized by sharing the power of GIS and human geography with both seasoned professionals and those just discovering its potential. I believe in starting with meaningful questions: identifying thematic, environmental, and human gaps in understanding, then crafting tailored solutions that are as effective as they are innovative. I bring a strong background in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), data security, ArcGIS Enterprise, and the broader ArcGIS system. But beyond the tech, my real strength lies in translating complex ideas into clear, actionable insights for any audience. Whether I’m leading technical teams or mentoring the next generation of GIS professionals, I’m driven by a desire to continuously grow, solve problems at scale, and help others succeed through thoughtful, strategic use of geospatial technologies. Let’s build something impactful together.
